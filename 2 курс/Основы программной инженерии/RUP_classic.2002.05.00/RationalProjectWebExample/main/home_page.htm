Purpose of this Home Page

The purpose of this page is to be the home page of your project, and you find it in file project/main/home_page.htm. Suggestions of what you might want to put on it:

  • News regarding the project
  • Project status reports
  • Meeting schedule, links to agendas, minutes
  • Demo schedule
  • Thank you's to team members who have done extraordinary efforts
  • New members on the team -- or thank you's to those who leave
  • Team members' happy birthday wishes or other such occasions
  • Book reviews
  • Seminar or course reviews/recommendations
  • ...

The file with the frames structure is called project/index.htm.

What might also be useful is a search engine, either directly on the home page, or on a linked page. (The treebrowser entry for search is set to this page with a bookmark in this paragraph for search.) This is just an example of a search form. You must replace this with a search form that works with your webserver. Talk to your local webmaster.
