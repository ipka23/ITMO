Templates 

This web site contains a Guidelines section (click here to view the section) which is organized around the Disciplines of the process.  This section contains pages providing access to, and instruction on using, the various sets of guidelines.

The guidelines themselves can be presented as word documents, single html pages or networks of html pages.  The Rational Unified Process identifies and provides templates for a number of guideline documents.  The table below provides access to the RUP: Guideline templates.

Guideline Templates
Business Modeling Guidelines RUP Template: Business Modeling Guidelines.
Design Guidelines RUP Template: Design Guidelines.
Manual Styleguide  None
Programming Guidelines RUP Template: Programming Guidelines
Test Guidelines RUP Template: Test Guidelines
Use-Case Modeling Guidelines RUP Template: Use-Case Modeling Guidelines.
User-Interface Guidelines None

Any additional guidelines required to support the process configuration should be documented in a similar style.

The guidelines should be presented as part of the process configuration.  The templates for these artifacts are less important than the majority of the other artifact templates as they are not instantiated by the projects.
