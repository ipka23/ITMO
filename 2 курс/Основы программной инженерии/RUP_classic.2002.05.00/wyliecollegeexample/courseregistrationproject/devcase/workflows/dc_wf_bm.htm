Notes on the Artifacts 

This project uses only one Glossary (defined in the Requirements Artifact Set) for describing terms from the Business domain in general as well as from the problem domain of the Course Registration System.

Prior to this project, Wylie College has completed an extensive Business Analysis process,  which means that the following artifacts will not be developed in this project : Target-Organization Assessment, Business Architecture Document, Business Rules, Business Vision.
